The practical parts

Shipping

I dispatch ready-to-ship pieces within 2–3 working days, wrapped by hand and sent tracked. Made-to-order and reserved pieces take longer — the timeframe is noted on the piece, and I'll always confirm by email before starting. UK and international shipping available; duties on international orders are the buyer's responsibility.

Returns

Because most pieces are one-off or small-batch, I ask that you choose carefully. Unworn, undamaged pieces can be returned within 14 days of delivery for a refund, minus the original postage — just message me first. Made-to-order and personalised commissions are non-returnable unless they arrive faulty. If something is wrong, tell me and I'll make it right.

Earrings & hygiene

Each pair of earrings is prepared with care and intended for immediate wear. For hygiene reasons, earrings are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged, whether purchased online or in person. I encourage you to review materials and details before buying, and to take your time when viewing pieces at markets or pop-ups. If an item arrives faulty, this will of course be resolved in line with your statutory rights.

Privacy

I only collect what I need to fulfil your order and reply to your messages: your name, email, and shipping address. Payments are handled entirely by PayPal — I never see or store your card details. I don't sell or share your information, and I won't add you to any mailing list unless you ask. Write to me any time to have your details removed.
