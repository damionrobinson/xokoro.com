About

The hand behind Xokoro

I'm a maker working alone from a small studio. I came to jewellery sideways, through colour — the dyed enamels, glass, and stones I couldn't stop collecting — and stayed for the discipline of paring each piece back until only the essential form remains.

Everything is made in short runs or as one-offs. I post new work almost daily, so the feed is less a catalogue than a record of what I'm thinking about that week. If a piece speaks to you, it may not be there tomorrow.

For commissions, gifts, or anything you're curious about, write to me below. I read every message myself.
